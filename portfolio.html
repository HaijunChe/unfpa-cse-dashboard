Project 02

Air Quality & Health Risk Monitoring

Identifying environmental signals as health risk indicators for data-scarce populations

Seasonal Pattern Analysis
Predictive Lag Analysis (3-week lead)
Comprehensive Environment-ILI Correlation
Model Residual Analysis

Tracks air quality and meteorological data as early signals for ILI outbreaks in Karachi. Key finding: temperature and NO₂ predict ILI with 3-week lead (r=-0.419, p=0.0001). Cross-indicator analysis transferable to CSE programme monitoring.

Tools: Python · pandas · matplotlib · scikit-learn · Gradient Boosting · XGBoost · R
